Some coworkers created a new company website based on node.js, express, etc. It's nice and all, and I love me some node.js but it's a bit overkill. So I forked it and converted it to angular.js as a SPA (Single Page App). It's based on the ng-boilerplate project, so you might see some references that project in the code.

Our company does a lot of geospatial stuff, so I thought it was appropriate to put a map on our website, so my fork has a map of all our our domestic corporate locations. :p
